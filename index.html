What is the difference between an Early Alert and Dispute?

Early Alert is a pre-dispute notification solution offered by Payoneer, designed to help merchants to proactively resolve customer issues before they escalate into chargebacks. If you refund the transaction based on these alerts, then chargeback is avoided. This helps you reduce your chargeback rates which is a very important KPI to keep your business running smoothly. Whereas a Dispute actually starts with the standard chargeback process wherein you are suppose to submit evidence documents in case you disagree with it. Early Alert happens first and Disputes later in case you did not refund the transaction based on the Early Alert. The sequence of Early Alerts and Dispute is explained in detail in Early Alerts | Early Alerts Process Flow . Note that, not all Disputes will have an Early Alert as a previous event.

拒付预警和拒付之间有什么区别？

预警是 Payoneer 提供的一种争议前通知解决方案，旨在帮助商家在客户问题上升为拒付之前主动解决问题。 如果您根据这些预警为交易退款，则可以避免拒付。 这有助于降低您的拒付率，这是一项非常重要的关键绩效指标（KPI），可以确保您的业务顺利进行。 实际上，拒付是从标准的拒付流程开始的，这一流程中，如果您不同意拒付，则您应提交证据文件。 如果您没有根据拒付预警进行交易退款，则会先发出拒付预警，随后产生拒付。 拒付预警和拒付的顺序在拒付预警 | 拒付预警流程中有详细解释。请注意，并非所有拒付都提前发出拒付预警。
